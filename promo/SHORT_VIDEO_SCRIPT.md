# SHORT VIDEO SCRIPT — AvtoNetGarage

**Format:** 30-second screen recording of Chrome browser on avto.net + extension popup
**Tone:** Confident, problem-solving — this is a tool for smart buyers, not casual browsers

---

## Hook — 0 to 5 seconds

**Screen shows:** A real avto.net vehicle listing page — a BMW 3 Series, €5,900, 187,000 km, year 2014. The floating "Shrani v Garažo" button is visible in the bottom-right corner of the page, subtly animated (pulsing or with a slight glow to draw the eye).

**Caption overlay:** "Shopping on avto.net? You're doing it wrong."

---

## Show — 5 to 20 seconds

**Screen shows (in sequence):**

1. Mouse moves to the floating button and clicks it. A brief checkmark animation plays: "Shranjeno!" (Saved!). The button changes state to "V Garaži" (In Garage).

2. Click the AvtoNetGarage extension icon in the Chrome toolbar. The 700×620px popup opens with dark glassmorphism styling. The BMW listing card is visible with:
   - Make/model, price (€5,900), mileage (187,000 km)
   - A Smart Advisor badge: "Score: 42 — Preverite" (Check it) in amber

3. Click the score badge — Smart Advisor panel expands:
   - Red flags: "Cena 21% pod povprečjem tržišča" (Price 21% below market average), "Visok kilometraž" (High mileage)
   - Positives: "Vozilo pregledano letos" (Vehicle inspected this year), "En lastnik" (One owner)

4. Click "Zgodovina cen" (Price History) tab — a line chart appears showing: saved at €6,500, dropped to €6,200, then €5,900. Three data points. Downward trend is clear.

**Caption overlay during price history chart:** "It's been dropping for 3 weeks. Now you know."

---

## Close — 20 to 30 seconds

**Screen shows:** The popup switches to the folders view — two folders visible: "Pod 6000€" and "Moj Shortlist" with custom colours (teal and amber). The BMW card is dragged into "Moj Shortlist" with a satisfying drop animation.

A Chrome desktop notification slides in from the top-right corner: "Golf 7 GTI — cena padla z €9,200 na €8,700 na avto.net"

**Caption overlay:** "Set it. Watch it. Buy smart. Free Chrome extension."

**Final frame:** Extension popup screenshot + "AvtoNetGarage" name + GitHub link.

---

## Production Notes

- Must record on actual avto.net — the floating button injection is the most compelling visual and it only appears on real listing pages
- Pre-populate the garage with 3-4 saved listings before recording so the popup doesn't look empty
- The price history chart needs at least 3 data points to look meaningful — inject fake history into chrome.storage before recording
- Glassmorphism UI requires a dark browser background or wallpaper to render correctly — set Chrome to dark mode before recording
- Record at exactly 700×620px popup size or slightly wider to capture the full popup without clipping
